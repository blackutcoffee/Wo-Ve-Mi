About Us

Wo-Ve-Mi is a website created by a diverse group of web designers with the goal of providing a way for people to search for women, veteran, and minority owned businesses in order to close the racial wealth gap, strengthen local economies, foster job creation, celebrate culture, honor our veterans, and hold larger companies accountable by giving them local competition for goods and services. Supporting local businesses is good for the environment, good for the economy, and provides local job creation.
